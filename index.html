About BRAINITA

BRAINITA (symbol: BRT) is a community-driven meme token built on the Solana blockchain. Our mission is to create a resilient, transparent, and fun token economy that supports creative community events, meme campaigns, and small utilities for holders.

Project vision

We believe in grassroots growth: strong community, clear on-chain information, and simple token mechanics. BRAINITA is positioned as a community treasury-backed meme project with emphasis on fairness, transparency, and long-term sustainability.

Crocodile brand

The BRAINITA crocodile is a playful symbol — bold, curious, and ready to explore Web3. Visual identity focuses on a friendly mascot, clear logo assets, and consistent branding across social channels.
